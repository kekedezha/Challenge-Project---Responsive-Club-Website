Welcome to Slow & Steady Run Club!

We're a friendly group of people based in Charlotte, NC who love to run. Trail running, road running, ruck running...whatever type of running you're into we have members who love to do the same! We are always looking to welcome more people into the group, because running is always better with company. Regardless, if you're an experienced runner or are new into running, whether you consider yourself fast or slow, we welcome all! Remember slow and steady wins the race! :)
